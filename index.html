I'm a software engineer and human-computer interaction researcher. I graduated from Brown University in 2020 with in Sc.B. in Computer Science, where my research was advised by Professor Jeff Huang at Brown HCI. I was an investigator on Self-E, an automated system that leverages remotely-captured user data to scaffold self-discovery and n-of-1 experimentation. Our paper, on which I was a second co-author, was accepted at ACM CHI 2021.

Currently, I am at the Asian American Studies Center at UCLA. We're uplifting Asian American and Pacific Islander histories by building an open-access multimedia digital textbook to be used in high school curriculums. Previously, I have worked at Meta, Beeswax (acquired by FreeWheel), and Anomalo. I'm also a proud alum of the Recurse Center!
